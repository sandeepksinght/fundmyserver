How It Works

For Project Creators (Seekers)

1. Submit your project with a clear breakdown of your essential monthly server/API costs.

2. We vet your project and costs for transparency.

3. Get matched with sponsors willing to cover your costs for a defined period (e.g., 3-6 months).

For Supporters (Sponsors)

1. Browse vetted indie projects needing operational funding.

2. Choose a project and sponsor its specific, itemized costs for a fixed term.

3. Make a tangible impact and help a promising project stay online.
